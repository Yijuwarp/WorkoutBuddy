## Full description (max 4000 characters)

Workout Buddy is a smart, no-nonsense workout tracker that adapts to you — recommending weights, reps, and rest based on your actual progress, not generic templates.

**Adaptive workout generation**
Every workout is generated from your strength, stamina, and available equipment — so you're never stuck doing a barbell exercise when you only have dumbbells at home. Tell Workout Buddy what equipment you own and it builds your sessions around it.

**Smart recommendations that grow with you**
Starting weights, reps, and durations are calculated from your strength/stamina scores and physical stats, then refined automatically as you log real sets. No spreadsheets, no guesswork.

**Rest timers and exercise timers that don't get in your way**
Automatic rest timers after each set (toggle on/off anytime), countdown timers for timed holds and cardio, with minimized banners so you can keep browsing your workout while they run.

**Track real progress**
- Personal records (PRs) tracked per exercise, with a celebration when you break one
- Strength and stamina scores that move based on real performance, not just time spent
- 8-tier rank progression (Bronze through Legend) so you can see how far you've come
- Full workout history with calendar view, calories, volume, and per-exercise breakdowns

**Built for how you actually train**
- Sort and filter the exercise library by muscle group, equipment availability, how often you've logged it, or how recently you've done it
- Create your own custom exercises
- Cardio, lifting, and timed-hold exercises all supported with type-appropriate tracking (distance/pace, weight/reps, duration)

**No accounts, no ads, no data collection**
Workout Buddy works entirely offline. Your data stays on your device — nothing is uploaded, tracked, or sold. No sign-up required, ever.

Whether you're just starting out or you've been lifting for years, Workout Buddy adapts to your equipment, your body, and your progress — not the other way around.
